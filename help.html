What is Value Betting?

Value is one of the most misunderstood terms in betting. For many punters, it’s about Backing things at big odds. If your average punter talks about something “being a value bet”, it generally means they are Backing an outsider. Just because an outcome is 34.0 (2.94% Probability), doesn’t make it value. Not if it should be 150.0 (0.67% Probability) in the market. (betfair, n.d.) Source: https://www.betfair.com.au/hub/betting-better/value-betting/
